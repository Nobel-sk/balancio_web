Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Payment Processing

Payments for Balancio subscriptions ($5/month Basic or $10/month Premium) are processed securely through Paddle, our authorised Merchant of Record. Prices may be lower in certain countries — the price shown in the app at checkout is the price you will be charged.

14-Day Refund Window

In accordance with Paddle's Buyer Terms, customers have the right to request a full refund within 14 days of purchase without providing any reason.

To request a refund within this period, use the in-app refund form under Settings → Help & Refund, or email us directly at balanciosupport@gmail.com .

After 14 Days

After the 14-day refund period has passed, refunds may be considered only in cases of:

  • Duplicate charges
  • Incorrect billing amounts
  • Technical issues preventing access to the service

Refund requests must include proof of purchase — your Paddle transaction ID or receipt email.

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time from within the app. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You will retain full access to your plan until that date.

How Refunds Are Processed

Approved refunds are processed through Paddle and returned to the original payment method used at checkout. Processing typically takes 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.

Contact

Questions about a refund? Email us at balanciosupport@gmail.com and we'll respond within 1 business day.
